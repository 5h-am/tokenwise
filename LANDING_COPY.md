## FAQ

### Is this actually free?

Yes. The audit is free to run. The business reason is simple: if the audit finds meaningful savings, Credex may be able to help you buy AI credits more efficiently. If it does not find much, you still get a useful report.

### Do you store my billing data?

The app stores the audit inputs you submit and the generated report. You do not upload invoices or connect billing accounts. For production, avoid entering sensitive internal notes into free-text fields unless the privacy policy explicitly covers them.

### What do you do with my email?

Your email is used for follow-up about the audit and Credex consultation requests. It should not be sold or used as a generic newsletter trap. If Sham changes that policy, the landing page should say so plainly.

### How accurate are the savings estimates?

The estimates are directional, not a replacement for checking your admin consoles. The engine compares your submitted spend, plans, seats, and usage context against known pricing and simple waste rules. It is strongest at spotting obvious issues: unused seats, team plans for solo users, redundant tools, and token-efficiency gaps.

### What is Credex and why are they offering this free?

Credex helps companies buy AI credits and related AI infrastructure more efficiently. A free audit is a useful front door because teams with messy AI spend are often the same teams that could benefit from better credit purchasing.

### Do I have to connect my accounts?

No. The current product is manual entry only. That makes the first audit faster and safer, but it also means the result is only as accurate as the numbers you enter.
